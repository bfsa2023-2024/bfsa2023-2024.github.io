Scholarships Information

The Science and Technology Scholarship was established in 2005. The scholarship aims to encourage and promote excellence in these fields, and to provide financial assistance to students who show great potential and promise.
The scholarship is open to all students who are enrolled in a science or technology program at an accredited college or university. Candidates for the scholarship are evaluated based on academic merit, demonstrated leadership and commitment to their field of study, and financial need.
Recipients of the scholarship receive a monetary award of 25,000 pesos per academic year, which can be used to cover tuition, books, and other educational expenses. The scholarship is renewable for up to four years, provided the recipient maintains satisfactory academic progress.
Since its establishment, the Science and Technology Scholarship has supported over 100 students in their pursuit of a career in science and technology. The scholarship has enabled these students to pursue their passions, achieve their goals, and make meaningful contributions to the scientific community.

List of Scholarships

  • Science and Technology Scholarship - Awarded to students who excel in science and technology.
  • STEM Scholarship - Awarded to students pursuing studies in Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics.
  • Research Scholarship - Awarded to students who have demonstrated potential in scientific research and innovation.
  • Women in Science Scholarship - Awarded to female students who have shown excellence in the sciences.
  • Minority in Science Scholarship - Awarded to students from underrepresented minority groups pursuing science studies.
  • Future Scientists Scholarship - Awarded to students who show great potential and passion for the sciences.
